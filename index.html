Example use cases

A few ways MapOps lands value across go-to-market motions.

Whitespace finder

Blend TAM layers with your accounts to highlight high-density target pockets your reps aren’t touching yet.

Drive-time routing

Auto-cluster leads into day-routes (e.g., 30–45–60 min rings) so field reps can stack meetings efficiently.

Territory re-cut

Rebalance books using opportunity density + existing pipeline so coverage matches reality, not zip-code myths.

Event radius blitz

Planning a conference? Pull a contact heat map around the venue, pre-book meetings, and send geo-personalized outreach.

Competitor overlap analysis

Identify where competitor presence is strongest and overlay your active accounts to uncover head-to-head opportunities.

Market expansion readiness

Layer incentives, solar adoption, or demographic fit to pinpoint ideal pilot regions before committing resources.

FAQ

How fast can we see a first map?

Typically within 48–72 hours once we have your data sample and goals.

What data do we need to start?

A CSV export or API access to leads/accounts with basic fields: name, address (or lat/long), segment, and owner (optional).

Who maintains the maps?

We can manage updates, or we’ll train your team to self-serve with MapOps templates.

Is our data private?

Yes — privacy by design. You control hosting. No cookies or trackers on your viewer links.

Can MapOps integrate with my CRM?

Yes. We connect via API or secure CSV import for Salesforce, HubSpot, and most major CRM systems.

Do you offer training or onboarding?

Absolutely. Our enablement sessions teach your leaders how to build and interpret maps for ongoing use.
